Illuminating Truth in the Digital Age

Project Overview

LUMINARA is an advanced artificial intelligence system designed to detect and classify AI-generated images. In an era where synthetic media is increasingly sophisticated, this project aims to provide reliable detection capabilities while promoting transparency and authenticity in digital content. The system leverages state-of-the-art deep learning architectures to analyze visual patterns and identify characteristics unique to AI-generated imagery.

Educational Purpose & Benefits

LUMINARA is developed for educational and research purposes to advance understanding in AI-generated content detection. This tool serves as a valuable resource for researchers, educators, and students studying machine learning, computer vision, and digital media authentication. By providing transparent evaluation metrics and accessible technology, LUMINARA contributes to the broader scientific community's efforts in maintaining digital content integrity and fostering critical thinking about AI-generated media.

Applications & Objectives

Media Verification

Assist journalists and fact-checkers in verifying the authenticity of digital images in news reporting and investigative journalism.

Educational Research

Provide a platform for academic institutions to study AI-generated content patterns and develop counter-detection methodologies.

Content Moderation

Support social media platforms and content hosting services in identifying synthetic media for proper labeling and transparency.

Digital Forensics

Aid forensic analysts in investigating cases involving manipulated or synthetic visual evidence.

Project Objectives

  • Develop robust detection algorithms capable of identifying diverse AI-generated image types
  • Maintain high accuracy across various image resolutions and compression formats
  • Provide interpretable results with confidence scores for informed decision-making
  • Contribute to open research in AI detection and digital media authentication
  • Foster awareness about the capabilities and limitations of synthetic media detection

Tools & Technologies

PyTorch

Primary deep learning framework used for model development, training, and inference. PyTorch provides dynamic computational graphs and extensive GPU acceleration support, enabling efficient neural network operations.

EfficientNet B4

State-of-the-art convolutional neural network architecture serving as the backbone. EfficientNet B4 balances model depth, width, and resolution for optimal accuracy and computational efficiency.

Timm Library

PyTorch Image Models library providing pretrained model architectures and utilities. Timm facilitates transfer learning and offers standardized implementations of modern computer vision models.

Torchvision

Computer vision toolkit for PyTorch containing image transformation utilities, data augmentation techniques, and preprocessing pipelines essential for robust model training.

PIL (Pillow)

Python Imaging Library for image processing operations. Handles image loading, format conversion, and basic manipulation tasks in the preprocessing pipeline.

NumPy

Fundamental package for numerical computing in Python. Supports array operations, mathematical functions, and efficient data manipulation throughout the pipeline.
